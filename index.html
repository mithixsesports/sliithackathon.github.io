ABOUT HACKATHON

A hackathon is an event, typically lasting several days, where people, often working in teams, come together to collaboratively work on software projects. These projects can range from creating new software applications, developing hardware solutions, designing websites, or solving particular problems using technology.

Hackathons are characterized by intense, focused work periods where participants brainstorm ideas, rapidly prototype solutions, and often compete for prizes or recognition. The atmosphere is usually highly creative and collaborative, with participants sharing knowledge, skills, and resources to bring their ideas to life within a limited timeframe.

Hackathons are not only about coding; they also often involve various disciplines such as design, project management, and business development.
